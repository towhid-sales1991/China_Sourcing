Reading the Ledger

How this replaces the spreadsheet

Both source files — toys and stationery — are indexed in full below, with nothing summarized away. Each card carries the same fields the original files held.

  • Search matches product name, factory, and 1688 keyword at once.
  • Toy cards reveal margin and seasonal demand; stationery cards reveal target customer.
  • Category filters and search combine — narrow by both at the same time.
  • Everything renders client-side from the embedded dataset, so it works offline once loaded.
